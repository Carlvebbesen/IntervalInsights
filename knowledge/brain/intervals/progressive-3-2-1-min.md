# 5 × (3-2-1 min) progressive

> Five sets of descending 3-2-1 min efforts with a 60 s jog recovery, getting progressively faster.

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

```interval
discipline: run
reps: 5 sets of (3, 2, 1 min)
work: 3 / 2 / 1 min
work_intensity: progressive — "should go a bit faster" as reps shorten, toward 10k/VO2max
recovery: 60 s jog (joggepause)
recovery_intensity: easy jog
sets: 5
target: vo2max
notes:
```

## Purpose

- Develops `[[vo2max]]` with a large total volume of hard running; the descending structure
  rewards negative-split pacing. Pace refs in `[[pace-terminology]]`.
