# 4 × 2000 m

> Four 2 km reps — a classic threshold-to-VO₂max session depending on pace chosen.

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

```interval
discipline: run
reps: 4
work: 2000 m
work_intensity: threshold → 10k pace
recovery: easy jog (unspecified in note)
recovery_intensity: easy
sets: 1
target: lactate-threshold / vo2max
notes: pace toward 10k makes it more VO2max-oriented
```

## Purpose

- Versatile: at threshold pace it develops `[[lactate-threshold]]`; nearer 10k pace it
  pushes `[[vo2max]]`. Pace refs in `[[pace-terminology]]`.

## The double-threshold morning session

- The controlled cousin is **6 × 2000 m at 2–3 mmol/L** — Bakken's **Tuesday-morning** long
  sub-threshold session in a `[[double-threshold]]` day (`[[src-bakken-norwegian-model-revisited]]`):
  more reps, *easier* than a hard 4 × 2000, deliberately capped to leave the legs fresh for the
  evening's short reps. Same building block as `[[10min-threshold-reps]]`.
