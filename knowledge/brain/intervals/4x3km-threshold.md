# 4 × 3 km (threshold)

> Four 3 km reps at half/marathon pace with short rest — a staple threshold-volume session.

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

```interval
discipline: run
reps: 4
work: 3 km
work_intensity: half-marathon → marathon pace
recovery: 2-3 min
recovery_intensity: easy
sets: 1
target: lactate-threshold
notes:
```

## Purpose

- Builds `[[lactate-threshold]]` and marathon-pace durability through repeated long reps.
  Pace refs in `[[pace-terminology]]`.

## Execution notes

- Hold half-to-marathon pace evenly across all four; keep rest tight (2–3 min).
