# 20 × 90 s @ 10k pace

> Twenty 90 s reps at 10k pace with a 30 s pause — a high-volume VO₂max session.

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

```interval
discipline: run
reps: 20
work: 90 s
work_intensity: 10k race pace (Z5)
recovery: 30 s
recovery_intensity: easy
sets: 1
target: vo2max
notes: short rest + 20 reps = large time at high intensity
```

## Purpose

- Sustained `[[vo2max]]` stimulus: 20 reps at 10k pace with only 30 s rest accumulates heavy
  high-intensity volume. Pace refs in `[[pace-terminology]]`.
