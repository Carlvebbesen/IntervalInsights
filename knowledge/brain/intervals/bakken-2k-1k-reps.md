# Bakken 5×(2km + 1km)

> Five sets of a 2 km then 1 km rep with short rests — a Marius Bakken threshold session.

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

```interval
discipline: run
reps: 5 sets of (2 km + 1 km)
work: 2 km, then 1 km
work_intensity: threshold (~marathon→half-marathon pace)
recovery: 2 min after the 2 km, 90 s after the 1 km
recovery_intensity: easy
sets: 5
target: lactate-threshold
notes: Marius Bakken session (marathontrainingschedule.com)
```

## Purpose

- Sustained threshold volume to raise `[[lactate-threshold]]`. The 2k+1k pairing lets you
  hold strong pace with controlled fatigue. See `[[pace-terminology]]` for pace refs.

## Execution notes

- Even pacing across all 5 sets; the short 90 s rest after each 1 km keeps it honest.
