# 3 × (6-4-2 min) progressive

> Three sets of descending 6 / 4 / 2 min efforts where pace climbs from marathon to 10k pace within each set.

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

```interval
discipline: run
reps: 3 sets of (6 min, 4 min, 2 min)
work: 6 / 4 / 2 min
work_intensity: 6 min = marathon pace, 4 min = half-marathon pace, 2 min = 10k pace
recovery: 90 s / 60 s / 60 s
recovery_intensity: easy
sets: 3
target: lactate-threshold → vo2max
notes: pace rises as rep shortens
```

## Purpose

- Spans `[[lactate-threshold]]` to `[[vo2max]]` within each set; trains gear changes and
  pace control. Pace refs in `[[pace-terminology]]`.
