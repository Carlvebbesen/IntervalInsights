# Descending 5-4-3-2-1 km (standing rest)

> A descending km ladder with 2 min standing rest, progressing from just under marathon pace to faster than marathon pace.

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

```interval
discipline: run
reps: 5-4-3-2-1 km (descending)
work: 5,4,3,2,1 km
work_intensity: starts ~7 s/km off MP, finishes 4-5 s/km faster than MP
recovery: 2 min standing (ståpause)
recovery_intensity: rest
sets: 1
target: marathon-pace / lactate-threshold
notes: progressive — get faster as reps shorten
```

## Purpose

- Marathon-specific threshold work that finishes faster than race pace, training pace
  judgement and `[[lactate-threshold]]`. Pace refs in `[[pace-terminology]]`.

## Execution notes

- Discipline early: open ~7 s/km slower than marathon pace, finish the 1 km ~4–5 s/km
  *faster* than marathon pace. 2 min standing rest (`ståpause`) between reps.
