# 25-45 × 45/15

> Many short 45 s on / 15 s off reps — a high-density VO₂max session (Billat/30-15 family).

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

```interval
discipline: run
reps: 25-45
work: 45 s
work_intensity: hard (VO2max effort)
recovery: 15 s
recovery_intensity: easy
sets: 1
target: vo2max
notes: short on/off keeps oxygen uptake high across many reps
```

## Purpose

- Maximizes time near `[[vo2max]]` via short work/short rest, accumulating large volume at
  high intensity. Pace refs in `[[pace-terminology]]`.

## Execution notes

- Build the rep count over a training cycle; 45 reps is a very large dose.
