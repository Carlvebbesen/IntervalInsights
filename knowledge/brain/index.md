## Principles — the coach's guardrails
- `[[polarized-80-20]]` — ~80% easy / ~20% hard; the hard part **defaults to controlled threshold** (Norwegian/pyramidal), not VO₂max-polarized.
- `[[progressive-overload]]` — increase stress gradually and systematically.
- `[[periodization]]` — phase training (base → build → peak → recover) to peak on time.
- `[[recovery-and-adaptation]]` — fitness is built during recovery, not the workout.
- `[[specificity]]` — you get better at what you practice; **threshold-first** base, race-pace late.
- `[[muscular-load-limits-training]]` — the muscular system caps quality; weigh effect vs muscular cost.
- `[[control-intensity-by-measurement]]` — control by lactate/HR/talk-test, not feel.
- `[[fuel-for-the-work-required]]` — match carbohydrate intake to the session's demand; don't fuel easy/short, do fuel long/quality.

## Concepts — what things are
- `[[training-zones]]` — 5-zone intensity model + the lactate-anchored threshold zones.
- `[[vo2max]]` — ceiling of aerobic power; plateaus, then economy/threshold take over.
- `[[lactate-threshold]]` — where lactate outpaces clearance; the 2–3 mmol/L "sweet spot."
- `[[aerobic-base]]` — capacity for sustained easy work; the foundation.
- `[[muscle-tone]]` — resting muscular tension; the real limiter of training load.
- `[[running-economy]]` — O₂ cost at a given pace; the lever once VO₂max plateaus.
- `[[fractional-utilization]]` — % of VO₂max held at threshold; predicts racing.
- `[[lactate-testing]]` — in-field lactate to set/police intensity; the 🟢🟡🔴 traffic light.
- `[[carbohydrate-fueling]]` — carbs are the fuel for long/hard work; g/h + g/kg/day targets that protect pace and quality.
- `[[multiple-transportable-carbohydrates]]` — glucose+fructose use two gut transporters → past the ~60 g/h ceiling to ~90 g/h.
- `[[hydration]]` — fluid/electrolyte balance; 0.5–1 L/h, replace 150% after, mind hyponatremia.

**Training-load modeling** (the PMC — hub: `[[performance-management-chart]]`)
- `[[performance-management-chart]]` — the fitness–fatigue chart that turns daily load into Fitness/Fatigue/Form trend lines.
- `[[training-stress-score]]` — TSS: one number for a single session's stress (1 h @ FTP = 100); "not all TSS is equal."
- `[[chronic-training-load]]` — CTL "Fitness": slow ~42-day load average; reference values + the ~6-week adaptation lag.
- `[[acute-training-load]]` — ATL "Fatigue": fast ~7-day load average; the build/taper driver.
- `[[training-stress-balance]]` — TSB "Form" = CTL − ATL; the +10/+25 race window, individual & "a rough approximation."

**Nutrition & health** (hub: `[[performance-nutrition]]`)
- `[[performance-nutrition]]` — adapt nutrition to the work; the root of the nutrition cluster.
- `[[energy-availability]]` — eat enough for the load; low EA (RED-S) is a core health guardrail.
- `[[protein-for-athletes]]` — 1.2–2.0 g/kg/day, distributed; food beats powder.
- `[[iron-status]]` — O₂ transport; endurance women at risk; test before supplementing.
- `[[bone-health]]` — loading + fuelling build bone; low energy availability is the main threat.
- `[[muscle-growth]]` — hypertrophy needs resistance training + a surplus + protein.
- `[[sleep]]` — the biggest recovery lever; ~7–10 h; hygiene over trackers.
- `[[antioxidants]]` — eat the produce; high-dose supplements can blunt adaptation.
- `[[alternative-diets]]` — low-carb/restrictive diets underperform for endurance.
- `[[lactose-intolerance]]` — tolerate some dairy; don't lose the calcium.
- `[[food-allergy]]` — diagnose first, then replace the lost nutrients.
- `[[gluten-and-celiac]]` — strict gluten-free only for celiac; still hit carbs.
- `[[probiotics]]` — gut/immune aid mainly for travel & post-antibiotics.

## Methods — how-to / protocols
- `[[norwegian-method]]` — ⭐ **brain default** — lactate-controlled, threshold-dominant training clustered into double-threshold days.
- `[[double-threshold]]` — two same-day threshold sessions (~6–8 h apart); the method's engine.
- `[[kenyan-training]]` — LT-centered "comfortably hard" base, run several times a week.
- `[[altitude-training]]` — moderate-altitude blocks + a timed descent for a threshold peak.
- `[[tapering]]` — race-week "formtopping": cut volume, keep intensity → freshness + ~2–3%.
- `[[heat-acclimatization]]` — ~2 weeks of heat exposure to race in the heat; sibling of altitude/time-zone.
- `[[strength-for-endurance]]` — heavy/explosive strength for economy & durability (not bulk).
- `[[polarized-training]]` — the polarized 80/20 distribution (*alternative* to the default `[[norwegian-method]]`).
- `[[fueling-by-session-type]]` — what each session type *is* + how much to fuel it (sprints → long run → racing).

**Nutrition & lifestyle**
- `[[meal-pattern]]` — 4 meals/day, ≤3–4 h apart, 7 food groups; the daily base.
- `[[pre-training-nutrition]]` — carb-rich, low-fibre meal 1–3 h before.
- `[[during-training-nutrition]]` — 30–60 g carb/h + 0.5–1 L/h for efforts ≥90 min.
- `[[post-training-nutrition]]` — 1–1.5 g/kg carb + 10–20 g protein + 150% fluid after.
- `[[glycogen-loading]]` — taper + ~10 g/kg/day carb to supercompensate for races ≥90 min.
- `[[competition-day-nutrition]]` — race-day & multi-heat fuelling; nothing new on the day.
- `[[dietary-supplements]]` — food-first; few aids work; mind doping/contamination risk.
- `[[travel-nutrition]]` — food-safety & gut prep for camps/competition abroad.
- `[[time-zone-acclimatization]]` — manage jet lag; OLT app for the light/sleep schedule.

## Intervals — reusable structured sessions
- `[[4x4-norwegian]]` — 4×4 min near VO₂max; sharpening "X element" (staple only under polarized).
- `[[10min-threshold-reps]]` — 4–6×10 min @ 2–3 mmol/L; double-threshold morning / Kenyan staple.
- `[[descending-km-float]]` — 7-6-5-4-3-2 km (±variant) with 1 km float. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[bakken-2k-1k-reps]]` — 5×(2km+1km), 2′/90″ rest; Marius Bakken threshold. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[descending-5-1-km-standing]]` — 5-4-3-2-1 km, 2′ standing, progressive. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[progressive-6-4-2-min]]` — 3×(6-4-2 min), MP→HMP→10k. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[fartlek-4-3-2-1-min]]` — fartlek 4×(4-3-2-1 min), P60 (+ Moroccan 6-5-4-3-2-1 variant). ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[1000m-reps]]` — 6-12×1000 m, 60-90″ rest (incl. double-threshold evening @ ~3.0). ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[1000m-300m-race-pace]]` — 4–6×1000 + 3–7×300 race-pace test (Kennedy/Bakken). ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[400m-reps]]` — 15-25×400 m, 40-45″ rest; VO₂max/speed (+ threshold variant). ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[45-15-intervals]]` — 25-45×45/15 s; high-density VO₂max. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[4x2000m-reps]]` — 4×2000 m, threshold→10k (+ 6×2000 double-threshold morning). ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[progressive-3-2-1-min]]` — 5×(3-2-1 min), 60″ jog, progressive. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[4x3km-threshold]]` — 4×3 km, 2-3′, half/marathon pace. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[descending-1600-400-track]]` — 1600-1200-800-600-400, shrinking rest; Kenyan/Moroccan. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[3-2-1-km-reps]]` — 2-3×(3,2,1 km), 2′ active pause (incl. logged paces). ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[20x90s-10k]]` — 20×90 s @ 10k, 30″ rest. ⚠️ advanced.
- `[[hill-reps]]` — short/steep hill reps; the weekly "X element."
- `[[waitz-25x300m]]` — 25×300 m, 15″ rest; Grete Waitz high-density set. ⚠️ advanced.
