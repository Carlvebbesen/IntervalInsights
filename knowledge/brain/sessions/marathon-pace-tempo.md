# 10 km Marathon-Pace Tempo ("draget")

> A continuous 10 km at marathon pace — a key sharpening session done ~2 weeks before a marathon.

> [!warning] Demanding session — high volume at intensity. Assumes a well-developed `[[aerobic-base]]` and consistent, high-mileage training. Not for beginners.

## Structure

- **Warm-up:** easy jog + strides.
- **Main set:** 10 km continuous at marathon pace (`mp`, see `[[pace-terminology]]`).
- **Cool-down:** easy jog.

## Targets

- Steady marathon effort (`[[training-zones]]` Z3–Z4 / `[[lactate-threshold]]` region).
- Placement: ~2 weeks before goal marathon as a confidence/pace-lock session.

## Fueling

- Top up carbohydrate **before** the session. Because it's run at marathon pace, use it to
  **rehearse race-day fueling** — on race day target **60–90 g/h** (`[[fueling-by-session-type]]`,
  `[[carbohydrate-fueling]]`).
